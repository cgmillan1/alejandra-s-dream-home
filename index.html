# Alejandra's Home Portfolio A personalized home-finder experience built by Cristian Millan, Real Brokerage Utah. Features: Tinder-style swipe interface, photo galleries, scoring, notes, must-haves checklist, side-by-side comparison, mortgage estimator (with Utah tax rates and S.B. 240 reminder), regional map view, and one-click PDF export for client summaries.
